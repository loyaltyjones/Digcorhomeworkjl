~About Us~

Welcome to LaughOverflow! We are an the enterprise for all things related to comedy. Established in 1969 by Richard Pryor LaughOverflow lies in the heart of the Oakland City community of Atlanta, Georgia. Struggling with being homesick and a myriad of health issues, Pryor decided to bring a bit of his Midwest comedic flavor to the South. In 2024, we celebrate Richard Pryor and his contribution to LaughOverflow in serving the city of Atlanta. He is succeded by comedy greats Eddie Murphy, Katt Williams, and David Chappelle. All of which are equal partners and investors of LaughOverflow.
